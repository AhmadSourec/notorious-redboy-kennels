NOTORIOUS REDBOY KENNEL BREEDINGS

AT NRK, we take great pride in preserving the great legacy of authentic pit bull bloodlines that originated in England and Ireland. Hundreds of years of breeding and selection have developed the dogs of today. American Pit Bull Terriers have spirit, loyalty, dedication and heart within the most athletic canine body known to man. The breed has a “never give up” attitude, often called gameness, which makes them always give 110%. A puppy from Notorious Redboy Kennels will be a strong foundation to establish or expand your kennel’s breeding program. Our dogs make extremely loyal family pets, and will excel in canine athletic contests and confirmation shows.
