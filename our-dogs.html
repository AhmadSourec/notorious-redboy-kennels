WALLACE'S NOTORIOUS MAXIMUS

NOTORIOUS MAXIMUS IS THE GREAT GRANDSON OF THE GREAT BOOMER ROM

MAXIMUS comes from Pit Island Kennels, an established leader in preserving this authentic lineage which can be traced back to the eighteenth century. We feel so fortunate to have been able to partner with them.

Maximus' bloodline was made possible by breeder Katie Marlowe in 1983, when she bred Fanny (a double bred granddaughter of Bass Tramp RedBoy) back to RedBoy. This resulted in Bingo, a PURE REDBOY dog! This is the bloodline that the followers of real Pitbulls revere and value so highly. The opportunity to own a dog with this pure a bloodline is unprecedented today. These are the antithesis of designer dogs and bully dogs. We take great pride in preserving AUTHENTIC PITBULLS for future generations.

WALLACE'S SHEBA

SHEBA is our awesome petite Hollingsworth dog...full of fire! Hollingsworth dogs make excellent family companions. They also excel at many canine athletic sports such as treadmill racing, dock diving, agility, high jump, and personal protection.

Sheba adores people, and is her owner's favorite companion. She is gentle with children, and is a caring mother who is very devoted to her puppies. Despite her good nature, she is the Alpha dog of our entire kennel. She is smaller than our other dogs, yet they all defer to her. Sheba is so obedient that she will sit and wait for her owners without being leashed, but will never back down when challenged by another dog. She has all of the best qualities a family pet could possess.
